- **Start Small**: Test with 100 builds first

## ğŸ†˜ Quick Troubleshooting

### Tournament Won't Start
- Check that at least one substat is selected
- Verify target builds > 0
- Ensure output directory exists

### Application Crashed
- Restart Tournament Pro
- Click "Resume Tournament" when prompted
- Your progress is saved!

### Results Look Wrong
- Verify substats are correctly selected
- Check weapon types match your intent
- Ensure multiplier is set appropriately

### Too Slow
- Reduce number of builds
- Lower substat multiplier
- Select fewer substats
- Increase worker threads
